My wife and I have lived here for over 2 years and are about to renew our lease for another year. Avalon Meydenbauer has been a great place live. Location is best as it is right in the heart of downtown Bellevue with the convenience of the downtown park, shopping, dining, entertainment, and cinemas all right across the street and, best of all, a Safeway with Starbucks right under us. The leasing office staff has completely changed since we first moved in and the new staff is always friendly and helpful, answering any and all questions we may have. We have never had any problems with picking up packages that were delivered to the office. The maintenance staff is also very friendly, greeting us in the hallways, and they are always timely with our maintenance requests. The fitness center is spacious and very convenient. We like the individual TVs for the treadmills/ellipticals. Parking in the 2-level garage has never been a problem for us. We wish there were more visitor parking spaces, but I guess we ve been lucky with the few spaces whenever we do have visitors. The unit we live in suits us very well. We are on the middle floor facing Safeway s front parking lot. We rarely ever hear our neighbors. Outside noise (sirens, grocery carts, etc.) is expected and doesn t bother us too much. The unit does get hot in the summer time so we thought a $300 portable air conditioner was a good investment. Overall, we are very pleased with Avalon Meydenbauer. With the awesome location and conveniences, it will be difficult for us to move out (when it comes time).
