Lived here for two years now. It had an amazing staff when I first started. Adam and Kim are both extremely kind and helpful, and working with them have been a pleasure. However, Adam recently left and Kim has been promoted, so you end up having to deal with Brittany a lot of the times.

Brittany, on the other hand, is not so friendly. Any questions you have for her seems to be a headache to her, and she just wants you to go away as soon as possible. The more time you spend in front of her desk seems to be directly proportional to how much stress you're causing her.

You spend a lot of time week to week interacting with the Metro 112 staff, and you used to feel like you're part of this big family, but it's feeling less and less so. I tend to avoid visiting the office if Brittany's the only one there, just because of how unpleasant the interaction with her tends to be.

If Brittany's the one that lead you on your apartment tour, I would recommend doing another tour with Kim or Julia! This is an otherwise nice place to live.
