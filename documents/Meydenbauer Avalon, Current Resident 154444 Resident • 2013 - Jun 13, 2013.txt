As others have said, Noisy building. I can hear my neighbors TV, people in the hall way, keys rattling.. etc.. On the bright side I can always find a parking spot and it feels very safe to walk around. Great location next to the mall and lot of restaurants near by. The best part is safeway downstairs. and the Gym is mostly empty at the times I've gone. Inside the apartment the paint is chipped, the materials used is cheap, the walls are thin, all appliances work as expected but don't expect luxury nice. I don't think there is AC in this building.. the One time I tried to turn it on all I got was heat. I have a small window that looks out into the courtyard. Great view but noisy if there are neighbors playing with their dogs in the courtyard. The doors are a bit tricky but once you learn the 3 main exit routes it is easier to get in and out. Elevators are super slow but they eventually turn up. For the price this place asks for and for what you are getting. I don't think it is worth it.
