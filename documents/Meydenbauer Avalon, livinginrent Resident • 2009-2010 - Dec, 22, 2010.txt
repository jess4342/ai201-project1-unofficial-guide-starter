I have lived in this place for more than a year now and I love it! - Location, location, location. You just cannot find a better area right in the heart of Downtown Bellevue and on top of a Safeway. Living on top of Safeway is a big luxury, I drop by any time to buy milk, bread or anything else I need. - New construction. The building is new, the walls are thick, I have never heard a peep from my neighbors (But I also live on the topmost floor facing the courtyard, so it is super quiet). The amenities inside the house are good and the kitchen looks posh. It is peak winter now and I have turned on my heater just a couple of times. The windows are air tight and provide great insulation. - Friendly staff. The staff is very helpful and resolve any problems quickly. - Parking. I have never had trouble finding easy parking with a compact car. (except on late nights on weekdays) - Online facilities. Rent/maintenance etc. can all be handled online. - Package handling. The staff take care of any Fedex packages etc you receive and handle it well. The only big negative in this place is visitor's parking. There is no way to open the garage door for your buddy sitting in your apartment. You have to go down to the garage, drive up to the garage entrance and open the door. I just ask my buddies to find parking outside closeby. Overall, a great place to live and you will enjoy it here!
