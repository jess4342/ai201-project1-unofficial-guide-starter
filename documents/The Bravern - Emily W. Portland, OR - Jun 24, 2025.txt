We enjoyed living at The Bravern for 2.5 years, but recently moved out after a proposed 17% rent increase. Unfortunately, the quality just doesn't justify the price. Elevators are often slow or out of service, the lobby lights have been broken for months, and the small gym and limited amenities (two tiny dog runs, two shared grills) are inadequate for a building this size. The concierge desk frequently closes early with the same vague "due to unforeseen circumstances" message -- it's frustrating and unprofessional. Constant nearby construction and street noise only add to the headache. For what they charge, you'd expect much better.
